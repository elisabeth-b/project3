100 CITY SQUARE CHARLESTOWN, MA

Sorelle at City Square offers a modern and airy space for a quick nosh, a thoughtful cappuccino served in cup and saucer, or a refreshing beer or glass of wine with a salad or sandwich. Marble and steel countertops, unique music mixes, wireless internet access and plenty of seating make this an inviting destination for the extended city lifestyle or the passing tourist. Design your own deli sandwich, select from our signature sandwiches (from lobster rolls to peking chicken wraps), or choose a warm, grilled or pressed sandwich such as our bistro ruben or pork cubano. Bulky salads, soup specials, breakfast sandwiches, and the renowned Sorelle scones, muffins and pastries round out the offerings.
